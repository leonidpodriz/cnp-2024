Conference Scope

The objective of this conference is to provide opportunities and platform for exchange of information on theoretical, scientific and practical achievements with the discussion on challenges encountered for researchers, scientists, scholars and engineers in the field of Network and Information Security.

Also, a great attention has to be payed to the 5G security and privacy aspects, which are one of the most importance nowadays.

Topics

  • CYBERSECURITY AND CRYPTOGRAPHY
  • MACHINE LEARNING AND TELECOMMUNICATIONS IN CYBERSECURITY
  • WIRELESS NETWORK PRIVACY
  • RISK MANAGEMENT AND ECONOMIC SECURITY
  • CYBERSECURITY AND COMPUTER SCIENCE EDUCATION
